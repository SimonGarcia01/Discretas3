# Grammar description

Look the "Grammar.tx" file in the python project for the description of the grammar.
Here, you can read a brief description about how we design the grammar.

* **Post**: is a text with the combination of some or all of these elements in its content.
* **URLS**: our links or urls are formatted in the form "<url>".
* **Hashtags**: are formatted in the form "#hastag".
* **Mentions**: are formatted in the form "@username".
* **Emojis**: are formatted in the form ":)" and others emojis.
* **Rawtext**: is the normal text, a sequence of alfanumeric and non alfanomeric characters. Rawtext also has white spaces.
* **Underlined**: are formatted in the form "{underlined}".
* **Superindex**: are formatted in the form "^(textToPutISuperIndex)".
* **Subindex**: are formatted in the form "_(textToPutISubIndex)".
* **Formula**: are formatted in the form "$formula$". The formula has operators.
* **Bold**: are formatted in the form "*"text"*".
* **Italic**: are formatted in the form "-text-".
* **Strikethrough**: are formatted in the form "~text~".
